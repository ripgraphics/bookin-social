## Getting Started

### Accessing the PMS
1. Log in to your BOOKIN.social account
2. Click on your avatar in the top-right corner
3. Select "Property Management" from the dropdown menu
4. You'll be taken to your role-specific dashboard

### Dashboard Overview
Your dashboard provides a quick overview of:
- Key statistics (properties, revenue, expenses, etc.)
- Recent activity
- Quick actions for common tasks
- Pending items requiring attention

## Property Owners Guide

### What You Can Do
As a property owner, you can:
- **Manage Properties**: View, create, edit, and delete your properties
- **Approve Expenses**: Review and approve expenses submitted by hosts
- **View Financial Statements**: Track revenue, expenses, and net income
- **Manage Invoices**: View all invoices for your properties
- **Track Payments**: Monitor payment history and outstanding amounts

### Managing Properties

#### Viewing Your Properties
1. Navigate to **Properties** from the dashboard or sidebar
2. You'll see a list of all properties you own
3. Click on any property to view details

#### Property Details
The property details page shows:
- **Basic Information**: Listing details, management type, settings
- **Assignments**: Hosts and co-hosts assigned to the property
- **Invoices**: All invoices related to this property
- **Expenses**: All expenses for this property

#### Creating a Property
1. Click **New Property** or **Create Property** button
2. Select a listing from your listings
3. Choose management type:
   - **Self-Managed**: You manage everything
   - **Host-Managed**: Host manages day-to-day operations
   - **Co-Hosted**: Multiple hosts share management
4. Set commission rates and fees
5. Configure payment terms
6. Click **Save**

#### Editing a Property
1. Navigate to the property details page
2. Click **Edit** button
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save**

#### Deleting a Property
1. Navigate to the property details page
2. Click **Delete** button
3. Confirm deletion
4. **Note**: This action cannot be undone

### Managing Expenses

#### Viewing Expenses
1. Navigate to **Expenses** from the dashboard
2. You'll see all expenses for your properties
3. Filter by:
   - Status (pending, approved, rejected, paid)
   - Property
   - Expense type (cleaning, repair, maintenance, etc.)
   - Date range

#### Approving Expenses
1. Navigate to a pending expense
2. Review the expense details and receipt (if provided)
3. Click **Approve** button
4. Expense status changes to "Approved"
5. You can now create an invoice or mark it as paid

#### Rejecting Expenses
1. Navigate to a pending expense
2. Review the expense details
3. Click **Reject** button
4. Enter a reason for rejection
5. Expense status changes to "Rejected"
6. Host will be notified (if notifications are enabled)

### Financial Statements

#### Viewing Statements
1. Navigate to **Statements** from the dashboard
2. Select a time period (month, quarter, year)
3. View:
   - Total Revenue
   - Total Expenses
   - Net Income
   - Breakdown by property

#### Understanding Your Statements
- **Revenue**: Income from rental invoices
- **Expenses**: Approved expenses for properties
- **Net Income**: Revenue minus expenses
- **Commission**: Host/co-host commissions deducted

## Hosts & Co-Hosts Guide

### What You Can Do
As a host or co-host, you can:
- **View Assigned Properties**: See properties you're assigned to manage
- **Create Operational Invoices**: Invoice property owners for services
- **Submit Expenses**: Request reimbursement for property-related expenses
- **Track Commissions**: Monitor your earnings from property management

### Managing Invoices

#### Creating Operational Invoices
1. Navigate to **Invoices** → **New Invoice**
2. Select the property you're managing
3. Choose **Operational** as invoice type
4. Add line items:
   - **Service**: Cleaning, maintenance, etc.
   - **Repair**: Fixes and improvements
   - **Maintenance**: Regular upkeep
   - **Custom**: Other services
5. Set issue date and due date
6. Enter customer details (property owner)
7. Click **Create Invoice**

#### Invoice Line Items
- **Description**: Clear description of the service
- **Quantity**: Number of units
- **Unit Price**: Price per unit
- **Total**: Automatically calculated

#### Editing Invoices
- You can only edit **unpaid** invoices
- Navigate to invoice details
- Click **Edit**
- Make changes and save

#### Deleting Invoices
- You can only delete invoices you created
- Navigate to invoice details
- Click **Delete**
- **Note**: Only unpaid invoices can be deleted

### Submitting Expenses

#### Creating an Expense
1. Navigate to **Expenses** → **New Expense**
2. Select the property
3. Choose expense type:
   - **Cleaning**: Cleaning services
   - **Repair**: Property repairs
   - **Maintenance**: Regular maintenance
   - **Utilities**: Utility bills
   - **Supplies**: Property supplies
   - **Other**: Other expenses
4. Enter description and amount
5. Upload receipt (recommended)
6. Set expense date
7. Click **Submit**

#### Expense Status
- **Pending**: Waiting for owner approval
- **Approved**: Owner approved, ready for payment
- **Rejected**: Owner rejected, check rejection reason
- **Paid**: Expense has been paid
- **Reimbursed**: You've been reimbursed

#### Editing Expenses
- You can only edit **pending** or **rejected** expenses
- Navigate to expense details
- Click **Edit**
- Make changes and save

#### Deleting Expenses
- You can only delete **pending** or **rejected** expenses
- Navigate to expense details
- Click **Delete**

### Viewing Your Commissions
1. Navigate to your dashboard
2. View **Total Commissions** stat card
3. Check commission breakdown in property details
4. Commission is calculated based on:
   - Commission rate set for your assignment
   - Revenue from rentals on assigned properties

## Guests Guide

### What You Can Do
As a guest, you can:
- **View Rental Invoices**: See invoices for your bookings
- **Make Payments**: Pay invoices online
- **Download Receipts**: Get receipts for payments
- **Track Payment History**: View all your payments

### Viewing Invoices

#### Invoice List
1. Navigate to **Invoices** from the dashboard
2. You'll see all rental invoices issued to you
3. Filter by:
   - Status (unpaid, partial, paid, overdue)
   - Date range

#### Invoice Details
Each invoice shows:
- **Invoice Number**: Unique identifier
- **Property**: The listing you booked
- **Dates**: Check-in and check-out dates
- **Line Items**: Breakdown of charges
- **Total Amount**: Total due
- **Payment Status**: Current payment status
- **Due Date**: When payment is due

### Making Payments

#### Pay an Invoice
1. Navigate to an unpaid invoice
2. Click **Make Payment** button
3. Enter payment amount (partial or full)
4. Select payment method
5. Enter payment details
6. Click **Pay**
7. Receive confirmation

#### Payment Methods
- **Stripe**: Credit/debit card (instant)
- **PayPal**: PayPal account
- **Bank Transfer**: Direct bank transfer
- **Other**: Contact host for alternative methods

### Payment History

#### Viewing Payments
1. Navigate to **Payments** from the dashboard
2. View all your payment history
3. Filter by date range
4. Download receipts

#### Downloading Receipts
1. Navigate to a payment
2. Click **Download Receipt**
3. PDF receipt will be generated
4. Save for your records

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Access PMS
**Problem**: "Property Management" link not in menu
**Solution**: 
- Verify you have a PMS role (owner, host, co-host, or guest with reservations)
- Contact support if you believe you should have access

#### Dashboard Not Loading
**Problem**: Dashboard shows "Loading..." indefinitely
**Solution**:
- Refresh the page
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser

#### Invoice Not Showing
**Problem**: Expected invoice not in list
**Solution**:
- Verify invoice was issued to you (guests) or for your property (owners)
- Check filters applied
- Verify invoice status
- Contact property owner/host if issue persists

#### Can't Submit Expense
**Problem**: Expense submission fails
**Solution**:
- Verify you're assigned to the property
- Check all required fields filled
- Verify amount is positive
- Try uploading receipt in different format

#### Payment Not Processing
**Problem**: Payment fails or doesn't update
**Solution**:
- Check payment method details correct
- Verify sufficient funds
- Try different payment method
- Contact support with payment ID

## Best Practices

### For Property Owners
- **Review Expenses Regularly**: Check pending expenses daily
- **Set Clear Policies**: Communicate expense approval policies to hosts
- **Monitor Statements**: Review monthly financial statements
- **Keep Records**: Download and save invoices and statements

### For Hosts/Co-Hosts
- **Submit Expenses Promptly**: Don't wait to submit expenses
- **Include Receipts**: Always upload receipts with expenses
- **Clear Descriptions**: Write detailed descriptions for expenses
- **Invoice Timely**: Send invoices at consistent intervals

### For Guests
- **Pay on Time**: Pay invoices before due date
- **Keep Receipts**: Download and save payment receipts
- **Review Invoices**: Check invoice details match your booking
- **Contact Host**: Reach out with questions before due date

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I edit an invoice after it's paid?
**A**: No, only unpaid invoices can be edited. Contact the issuer if changes are needed.

### Q: How long does expense approval take?
**A**: Approval time depends on the property owner. Most respond within 1-3 business days.

### Q: Can I delete a property with active reservations?
**A**: Properties with active reservations cannot be deleted. Contact support for assistance.

### Q: What if I can't see a property I should have access to?
**A**: Verify your assignment is active. Contact the property owner to confirm your role.

### Q: How are commissions calculated?
**A**: Commissions are calculated as a percentage of rental revenue based on your assignment rate.

## Glossary

- **Property Management**: System for managing property operations
- **Operational Invoice**: Invoice for property management services
- **Rental Invoice**: Invoice for guest bookings
- **Expense**: Cost incurred for property maintenance/operations
- **Commission**: Percentage of revenue paid to hosts/co-hosts
- **Property Assignment**: Link between a host/co-host and a property
- **Financial Statement**: Report showing revenue, expenses, and net income
